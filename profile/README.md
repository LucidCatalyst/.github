# LucidCatalyst

**LucidCatalyst is a consulting firm at the intersection of energy and climate, providing transformative solutions for the world's largest energy users.**

By combining technical expertise and strategic leadership with a deep understanding of what creates and drives social and technological change, LucidCatalyst delivers incisive analysis and powerful engagement to enable growth and energy security through strategic solutions.

## Our Focus

LucidCatalyst specializes in accelerating clean energy deployment, particularly nuclear power and industrial decarbonization. We work with industry, governments, and financial institutions to unlock scalable, evidence-based pathways towards abundant clean energy.

## Selected Work

- **[Open Architecture Nuclear Integration Framework](https://www.lucidcatalyst.com/open-architecture-nuclear)**: A white paper examining how standardized, modular approaches to nuclear system design can address clean energy challenges for industrial users.
- **[A New Nuclear World: How SMRs Can Power Industry](https://www.lucidcatalyst.com/post/new-study-from-lucidcatalyst-shows-significant-potential-market-for-smrs-providing-net-zero-potential-market-for-smrs-providing-net-zero-solution)**: Commissioned by Urenco, analyzing energy demands across 11 industries representing 80% of industrial energy use.
- **[Missing Link to a Livable Climate](https://www.lucidcatalyst.com/hydrogen-report)**: A landmark report on hydrogen-enabled synthetic fuels as a pathway to meeting the Paris climate goals.
- **[Beautiful Nuclear](https://www.lucidcatalyst.com/beautifulnucleardrivingdeepdecarbonisation)**: Demonstrating how nuclear energy supports all 17 UN Sustainable Development Goals.
